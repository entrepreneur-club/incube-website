About InCube

What happens, if you put interdisciplinary students inside a box, inject some entrepreneurial DNA and let them work on current challenges?

Next semester, the ETH Entrepreneur Club wants to find out! 3 teams work during 4 days, locked inside transparent cubes at 3 hotspots all over the city of Zurich.

The FAQ

What will I be doing at InCube?

You will be matched with other motivated people and placed in a glass cube for four days, where you will be trying to solve a challenge and find an innovative and interdisciplinary solution to it. Next to your main challenge you will also be surprised with some mini-challenges to gain new jokers and tools that help you develop your solution. At the end of the fourth day, you will present your solution to a jury and have the chance to win a lot of fame (and some prize money of course).

When and where will the event take place?

The event will start on Friday, 6th October 2017 in the morning and will end on Monday, 9th October in the evening. The locations will be three glass boxes distributed in hot-spots of the city of Zurich.

What are the challenges to solve?

Each cube will have a different challenge to tackle - either FinTech, EdTech or the future of mobility.

How much will it cost?

This event will be free for all participants! We will provide all the food and drinks during the four days as well as the accommodation a.k.a. the cubes. Also, you will be supplied with all necessary tools to support the realization of your prototypes.

What are these cubes like?

The cubes have a size of 20 m^2 and will be fully furnished, have appropriate air conditioning and a privacy corner where you can escape the glass walls and take your peaceful nap. You will have access to a toilet and showers in a nearby building.

Why glass?

Because we want you to interact with the people passing by. The glass stands also for transparency of new technologies with the public. Your work will hopefully inspire the people or at least ignite some thoughts.

Am I allowed to leave the cube?

As mentioned before, you will have 24 hour access to showers and toilets which are not part of the cube, but rather in the vicinity of the cube. Therefore, you are of course allowed to leave the cube. However, we want you to do all the work inside of the cube and you are also going to sleep there. The cube will be your office and bedroom for those four days!

What is the team size?

Each cube will be the workplace of 5 people.

Who can apply?

All students (BA, MA) and PhDs from Swiss universities are welcome to apply! If you are eager to experience a new, crazy challenge and have an entrepreneurial and open-minded spirit, then you are exactly who we are looking for.

How can I apply?

Applications are open now! Just fill out the application form here and you will be hearing from us soon.

What do I need to bring with me?

We just need your time and commitment for the whole four days. Also, you should bring your own computer with charger, as well as some replacement clothes. We will however inform you about any additional things when the event comes closer.

Why should I participate?

It will be an incredible experience with a lot of learnings and fun, and you will have the chance to be in contact with relevant industry partners. Also, the most promising team will get the potential for project incubation and support as follow-up - hopefully turning the ideas into startups?

Can I just come and watch the event?

Of course! Everyone is welcome to come and see from the outside what the three teams are doing. You are also welcome to join the closing ceremony, more information will follow on this website.
